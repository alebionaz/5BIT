What we Offer

Agricon Base

This is an Arduino board that is attached to an humidity and temperature sensor. Thanks to an LCD display you are able to see the valors.

Agricon Plus

This is an Arduino board that is attached to an humidity, temperature and Ethylene gas sensor. Thanks to this module you can connect to an apllication that will shows the valors directly on you phone.

Agricon Premium

This is an Arduino board that is attached to an humidity, temperature, Ethylene gas and wind sensor. Thanks to this module you can connect to an apllication that will shows the valors directly on you phone.
